üìä Dashboard Overview

Your agent dashboard provides a complete view of your sales performance, leads, orders, and commissions.

Your Key Metrics:

  • My Leads: Total leads assigned to you
  • My Orders: Leads you've successfully converted
  • Conversion Rate: Your success rate percentage
  • Total Commission: Your earnings from completed orders

üí° Quick Tip:

Check your dashboard daily to track new leads and follow up on pending orders. Consistent follow-up improves conversion rates!

üì¶ Tracking Your Orders

Converting Leads to Orders

Note: If you're an internal agent, you can convert leads directly. External agents should send qualified leads to admin for conversion.

1

Qualify the Lead

Ensure client is ready to proceed with installation

2

Click "Convert" Button

Available on qualified leads (internal agents only)

3

Enter Order Details

Service ID, Account Number, Order Number from Openserve

4

Confirm Conversion

Order is created and commission is calculated

Viewing Your Orders

  • Click "My Orders" to see all your converted leads
  • Track installation status and progress
  • View commission amount for each order
  • Filter by order status (Processing, Scheduled, Completed)

‚è∞ Your Preorders NEW

Preorders are leads where the client wants to order but installation is scheduled for a future date. Track them separately for better organization.

Viewing Your Preorders

1

Click "My Preorders" Tab

See all your leads marked as preorders

2

Filter by Status

View preorders by their current status

3

Follow Up Regularly

Keep in touch with preorder clients until installation date

üí° Visual Indicator:

Preorder leads show a purple badge with clock icon in your leads table, making them easy to identify at a glance.

üìã Note:

Only admins can mark leads as preorders. If you have a preorder client, inform your admin to flag the lead appropriately.
