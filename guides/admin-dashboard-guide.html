üìä Dashboard Overview

The admin dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your entire dealer network, leads, orders, and performance metrics.

Key Metrics Displayed:

  • Total Leads: All leads in the system (excluding converted)
  • Total Orders: Active orders being processed
  • Active Agents: Number of registered agents
  • Dealers: Total dealer count
  • Conversion Rate: Percentage of leads converted to orders
  • Total Commission: Sum of all commissions from orders

üí° Quick Tip:

Use the global search bar at the top to quickly find any lead by name, email, or phone number.

üì¶ Managing Orders

Converting Leads to Orders

1

Click "Convert" on a Lead

Opens the conversion modal with lead details

2

Enter Order Details

Service ID, Account Number, Order Number

3

Commission Auto-Calculated

Based on package pricing and dealer commission rate

4

Confirm Conversion

Lead status changes to "converted" and order is created

Order Statuses

Processing Order received, awaiting installation
Scheduled Installation date confirmed
Completed Installation successful, service active
Cancelled Order cancelled by client or admin

‚è∞ Preorders Feature NEW

Track leads that are preorders - clients who want to order but installation is scheduled for a future date.

Marking a Lead as Preorder

1

Open Lead Details

Click "View" or "Edit" on any lead

2

Toggle Preorder Switch

Purple toggle at bottom of modal - turn ON

3

Save Changes

Lead now appears with purple "Preorder" badge

Viewing All Preorders

  • Click "Preorders" tab in sidebar
  • View all leads marked as preorders in one place
  • Filter by status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted)
  • Export preorders to CSV for reporting
  • Remove preorder flag when ready to process normally

üí° Visual Indicator:

Preorder leads show a purple badge with clock icon in the leads table, making them easy to spot at a glance.
