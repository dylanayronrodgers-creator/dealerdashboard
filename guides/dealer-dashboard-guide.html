üìä Dashboard Overview

Your dealer dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your entire agent network, their leads, orders, and overall performance metrics.

Key Metrics Displayed:

  • Total Agents: Number of agents working under your dealership
  • Total Leads: All leads assigned to you and your agents
  • Total Orders: Successfully converted orders from your network
  • Conversion Rate: Overall success rate of your dealership
  • Total Revenue: Combined earnings from all orders

üí° Quick Tip:

Monitor your dashboard daily to track agent performance and identify top performers. Regular oversight helps maintain high conversion rates across your network.

üîß Common Questions

Q: Why don't I see all my leads?

A: Check your filters - you may have status or agent filters applied. Clear all filters to see complete lead list.

Q: How do I add a new agent?

A: New agents must register through the agent portal. Once registered, they'll appear in your "My Agents" section automatically.

Q: Can I edit lead details?

A: Dealers have view-only access to leads. Contact admin if lead information needs to be updated.

Q: How is commission calculated?

A: Commission is based on your dealership's commission rate multiplied by the package price. It's calculated automatically when a lead converts to an order.
